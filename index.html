Bringing an End to Homelessness

Homelessness in Indiana

Last year their were 4,398 people experiencing homelessness in Indiana. Of these people 804 of them didn't have a shelter to go to. On top of that 21% of these people were under the age of 18.

Homelessness across the U.S.

Compared to other states with a larger population indiana's homeless populaiton seems rather small. In california last year approximately 171,521 homeless peopled. Last year their were 653,104 people experiencing homelessness across the entire country.
